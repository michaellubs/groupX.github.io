Privacy Policy

At COCCIS Connect, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how
we collect, use, and disclose personal information from our users.

Information We Collect

- Personal information: name, email address, phone number, profile picture, and bio.
- Login information: username and password.
- Location information: city, country.
- Content shared on the platform: posts, images, videos, comments.

How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve our services.
- To authenticate your account.
- To respond to your support queries.
- To send important notices about our services.
- To comply with legal obligations.

Information Sharing

- We share personal information with third-party service providers.
- We share information to comply with legal obligations.
- We share information to protect our rights and property.

Data Security

- We use encryption to protect your data.
- We have implemented security measures to protect against data breaches.

Data Retention

- We retain personal information for as long as your account is active.
- We retain information for legal and audit purposes.

Your Rights

- You have the right to access and correct your personal information.
- You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.
- You have the right to object to processing of your personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

- We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
- We will notify you of significant changes by email or notification on our platform.

By using COCCIS Connect, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.
If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at mailto:support@coccisconnect.com.
