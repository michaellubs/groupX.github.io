Here are some Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for COCCIS Connect:




Q: What is COCCIS Connect?
A: COCCIS Connect is a social media platform designed specifically for students at Makerere University.

Q: How do I register for COCCIS Connect?
A: To register, simply go to the COCCIS Connect website or mobile app and sign up with your valid Makerere University email address.

Q: What kind of content can I share on COCCIS Connect?
A: You can share posts, images, videos, and other content that is appropriate and respectful. Please refer to our Terms and Conditions
for guidelines on acceptable content.

Q: How do I report inappropriate content or behavior?
A: If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our Terms and Conditions, please report it to our support team via the
"Report" button or by emailing mailto:support@coccisconnect.com.

Q: Is my personal information safe on COCCIS Connect?
A: Yes, we take privacy and security seriously. Our platform uses encryption and other measures to protect your personal information.

Q: Can I use COCCIS Connect on my mobile device?
A: Yes, COCCIS Connect has a mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices.

Q: How do I change my password or update my account information?
A: You can update your account information and change your password by logging into your account and accessing your profile settings.

Q: What if I forget my password?
A: If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Q: How do I contact the COCCIS Connect support team?
A: You can contact our support team via email at mailto:support@coccisconnect.com or through the "Help" section on our website or mobile app.
